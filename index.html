Our Story

Our story starts during the Covid-19 days, I loved cookies from childhood, but in the Covid-19 days, it was difficult to order delicious cookies! So I made my first cookie bake experience, which was a failure, and then I tried to do it repeatedly and learned from my mistakes until it was perfected in a balanced and delicious way.

After that, I used to offer it with all my love to relatives and friends, and it won praise, then they suggested that I launch a store that sells delicious cookies.
